### Project Name: KeenKeeper

📖 About The Project

**KeenKeeper** is a personal relationship management app built with React.  
Life gets busy — and friendships quietly fade when we stop being intentional.  
KeenKeeper solves this by letting you:

- 📋 Track all your important friends in one place
- ⏱️ See exactly how many days have passed since your last contact
- 🎯 Set personal contact goals for each friend
- 📞 Log calls, texts, and video chats with a single tap
- 📊 Visualise your relationship habits with analytics charts

✨ Key Features

### 1. 🟢 Smart Friend Status Tracking

Every friend card shows a live status badge — **On Track**, **Almost Due**, or **Overdue** — based on your last contact date and personal goal. A colour-coded border and dot make it instantly scannable at a glance.

### 2. ⚡ One-Click Quick Check-In Logging

On any friend's detail page, hit **Call**, **Text**, or **Video** to instantly log an interaction. It timestamps the entry, saves it to the Timeline automatically, and fires a toast notification — no forms to fill out.

### 3. 📊 Friendship Analytics with Interactive Charts

The Stats page shows a live **Recharts Pie Chart** breaking down your Call / Text / Video interactions. Hover any slice to see the exact count, percentage share, and a mini progress bar — plus individual progress bars for each interaction type.
